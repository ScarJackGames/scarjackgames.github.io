How to play

Drag coloured blocks from your containers onto the grid. Line up three or more of the same colour to clear them. Keep placing blocks next to matching colours to build your streak and multiply your score.

The Daily Challenge

Every day, all players get the same board in a two-minute time attack. Your score is submitted to a global leaderboard that resets each day. Come back tomorrow for a fresh board.

Leaderboards

BLOXL uses Apple’s Game Center. Make sure you’re signed in — Settings › Game Center — to submit scores and see the global rankings.

Progress and scores

My progress disappeared

Progress is stored on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, local progress may be lost. Game Center scores are tied to your Apple ID and will return when you sign back in.

A score didn’t save

Check that you’re signed in to Game Center. Scores submitted while signed out won’t reach the leaderboard.

Report a bug
