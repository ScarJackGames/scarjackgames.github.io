Privacy Policy

Last updated 18 July 2026

ScarJack Games (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the Bloxl app (“the App”) does and does not collect.

What we collect

Nothing is collected by ScarJack Games. We do not collect, store, or share personal information from BLOXL. The App does not require a ScarJack Games account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

What stays on your device

The App saves certain preferences and local game information — such as settings, high scores, and tutorial completion — on your device using Apple’s standard storage. This information is not transmitted to ScarJack Games.

Game Center

If you are signed in to Apple’s Game Center, BLOXL submits your scores to leaderboards so you can compete with other players. This is handled entirely by Game Center and is subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your Game Center identity.

Children’s privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
